What is Virtualecture?

Students in colleges spend over a hundred dollars on each lecture they are enrolled in. However, too often, real life gets in the way of attending class. Missing class for various reasons also poses an additional burden to instructors. Being able to remotely attend lectures would allow students to maximize their college education, while allowing professors to effortlessly ensure that more students are caught up in class.
